Traction lifts are slightly faster than hydraulic lifts – making them a better choice for very high buildings. Safety systems in a traction lift are designed specifically to prevent 'dead drops' and injury through mechanical failure or cables breaking

Higher, faster, smoother

Traction elevators move using a system of steel ropes or belts. This configuration enables them to move faster than hydraulic elevators. They can also travel longer vertical distances, making them ideal for mid-rise, high-rise, and even ultra-high rise buildings.

We offers a wide variety of traction elevators to meet your needs and specifications. And with a wide variety of fixtures and design options to choose from, you can customize your elevator to blend seamlessly with the rest of your building.

Traction Elevator Products

EOX

Eco-efficient and digitally native. EOX provides value at every level and is optimized to save you time and space. EOX provides a state of the art experience through the entire lifecycle of the elevator.

evolution 200

A gearless traction, machine room-less elevator. evolution 200 incorporates the highest engineering standards with a wide variety of options.

synergy

A gearless traction, machine room-less elevator with steel cables. In synergy, sophisticated components create a smooth ride, increase efficiency and improve reliability.

momentum

A gearless traction elevator with a machine room. Designed for high-rise buildings, momentum combines high-speed performance with proven safety features.

TWIN

An elevator with two cars operating independently in the same hoistway. TWIN’s innovative design allows you to transport up to forty percent more passengers than traditional elevators.

Traction Elevators comes in three types:


Geared machine room-less lifts, Gearless machine room-less lifts, Conventional traction lifts – Geared or Gear-Less

Faster, safer installation

We’ve streamlined the installation process to minimize disruptions and complete construction quicker. We’ve grouped similar tasks together, resulting in less coordination with other trades. Our goal is to efficiently install your elevators using sequential packaging strategies, being mindful to stage materials with the least amount of interference for other trades. We've also designed our elevators to enable early installation of the roof.
